How The Kickstarter Success App Works

Kick start your campaign with an edge up on the competition. Our app gives you detailed insight into building a successful Kickstarter campaign.

Kickstarter has opportunities for you in Arts, Comics & Illustration, Design & Tech, Film, Food & Craft, Games, Music, and Publishing. We have experience in each of these categories. Our experts can spark your creativity and help you save time and money on your Kickstarter campaign.

Our app uses machine learning techniques to analyze past data in order to predict the likelihood of success of your Kickstarter campaign.

Follow the steps below to get started.



Getting Started

  1. Go to the Kickstarter Success App and click the "Sign Up" link (shown above). The Sign Up page displays.

  2. Enter a username in the "username" field.

  3. Enter your valid email address in the email field.

  4. Enter a password in the password field (must be at least five characters).

  5. Click the "Sign Up" button to continue. The Login form displays.

  6. Login with the username and password you created. Your user home page displays.



Creating a New Campaign Prediction

  1. From your user home page, click the plus icon () in the upper left corner of your browser window. The Campaign Prediction form displays.

  2. Enter a campaign name.

  3. Enter a monetary goal.

  4. Enter a campaign length in number of days (cannot be more than 160 days).

  5. a main campaign category from the dropdown menu.

  6. a subcategory from the dropdown menu.

  7. Select your country of residence.

  8. Enter a campaign description.

  9. Click the "Predict Success!" button to continue. Your campaign prediction will be calculated. This may take a few seconds to update. Please be patient.
