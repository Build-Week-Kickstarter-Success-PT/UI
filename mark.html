What Is Kickstarter Success?

Kickstarter Success Predictior is an app that was designed to help individuals looking to start a campaign, predict the likliness of a successful campaign.

How It Works

Our Kickstarter Success app was created to allow users to have full control of their campaign predictions. By just creating an account you gain access to the many useful features of our app such as:

  • Ability to Create A Campaigns
  • Ability to Delete A Campaign
  • Manage Account Settings
  • Update Account Information
How Accurate Is It?

Kickstarter Success Preictor makes predictions based off of the aquired information of the account holder. Our app is designed to make a prediction solely off of this data to avoid mistakes.

Features of Kickstarter Success App
  • Data of Predictions From A Succesful Campaign
  • Login/Sign up Form
  • Prediction Data Form
  • Ability For Users To Manage Their Campaign Online
  • User Profile Page
